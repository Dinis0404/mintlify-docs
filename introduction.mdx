## New here? Start in 4 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read the rules">
    Before you do anything else, take a few minutes to read the [Server Rules](/getting-started/rules). Every player is expected to know and follow them — ignorance is not an excuse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the server">
    Fire up Minecraft: Java Edition and connect to **play.dirtshop.gg**. See the [Joining](/getting-started/joining) guide if you need help adding the server to your multiplayer list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your bearings at spawn">
    Once you're in, run `/spawn` to teleport to the main spawn area. From there you can access the player market, read information boards, and find warps to key locations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your first home">
    When you find a spot you like, run `/sethome <name>` to save it. You can teleport back at any time with `/home <name>` — so you never lose your base again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Stuck, confused, or just want to say hi? Join the [DirtShop SMP Discord](/community/discord) — the community and staff team are active and happy to help you get settled.
</Tip>
