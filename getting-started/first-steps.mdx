Your first day on DirtShop SMP sets the foundation for everything that follows. Taking a little time upfront to set your home, claim some land, and learn the basics of the economy will save you a lot of headaches later. This guide walks you through the essential first steps so you can focus on what matters most — playing and having fun.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set your home">
    The first thing you should do after finding a spot you like is save it as a home. Run:

    ```
    /sethome <name>
    ```

    Replace `<name>` with anything memorable — for example, `/sethome base` or `/sethome mine`. To teleport back to it at any time, run:

    ```
    /home <name>
    ```

    You can set multiple homes as you progress and unlock higher ranks. Lost track of your homes? Run `/home` with no arguments to see a list of all your saved locations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a place to build with /rtp">
    Spawn and the areas immediately surrounding it fill up quickly. To find fresh, untouched land, use the random teleport command:

    ```
    /rtp
    ```

    This teleports you to a random location in the overworld, far from spawn and away from existing player builds. Run it a few times until you land somewhere that looks promising — look for a biome you like, flat terrain for building, or proximity to resources you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim your land">
    Once you've found your spot and started building, protect it with a land claim. DirtShop SMP uses a **GriefPrevention**-style claiming system powered by the golden shovel.

    1. **Get a golden shovel** — craft one or find it in your starter kit.
    2. **Right-click one corner** of the area you want to claim.
    3. **Right-click the opposite corner** (diagonally across) to complete the claim.

    A glowing border of gold and glowstone blocks will confirm the claim is active. All blocks and chests within a claim are protected — other players cannot break them or access your storage.

    Run `/claiminfo` while standing inside a claim to see its details, or `/abandonallclaims` to remove your claims if you move on. You earn more claim blocks over time just by playing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn your first coins">
    The DirtShop SMP economy runs on **coins**, and there are several reliable ways to earn them on your first day:

    - **Mine and sell resources** — Head underground and mine stone, ores, and other materials. Many raw resources can be sold directly to the server shop at `/warp market`.
    - **Complete tasks and challenges** — Check `/quests` or `/tasks` for beginner-friendly objectives that reward coins on completion.
    - **Trade with other players** — Other players are often buying materials you might have in surplus. Ask in chat or check the market for buy orders.

    Even a short mining session can net you enough coins to buy starter gear or upgrade your tools through the player market.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visit the player market">
    The player market is the economic heart of DirtShop SMP. To get there, run:

    ```
    /warp market
    ```

    Here you'll find player-run shops selling everything from building materials and food to rare items and enchanted gear. Browsing the market is also a great way to see what's in demand — if a resource is selling for high prices, you've found yourself a business opportunity.

    When you're ready to open your own shop, see the [Economy & Shops](/gameplay/economy) guide for full instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Learn the available commands">
    There's a lot you can do on DirtShop SMP, and the best place to start learning is the in-game help system. Run:

    ```
    /help
    ```

    This lists the main command categories available to you. You can drill into any category — for example, `/help teleport` — to see a full breakdown of related commands and their syntax. For a comprehensive reference, check the [Commands Reference](/commands/general) page in this documentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
