DirtShop SMP is more than just a survival server — it's a living community that comes together regularly to compete, create, and celebrate. From adrenaline-fueled PvP nights to creative build contests and holiday spectaculars, there's always something on the horizon. This page covers every recurring event, how to get involved, and what you can win.

## Recurring Events

Events on DirtShop SMP run on a consistent schedule so you can plan ahead. Here's a breakdown of what happens and when:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Friday PvP Night" icon="sword">
    **Every Friday at 8PM EST.** Suit up and head to the PvP arena for a night of structured combat. Formats rotate weekly between free-for-all, team deathmatch, and kit-based tournaments. Everyone is welcome regardless of gear — kits are provided for kit rounds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build Contest" icon="hammer">
    **Bi-weekly (every other weekend).** A theme is announced in Discord and players have one week to submit their build. The community then votes on their favorites. Whether you're an architect or a first-time builder, every submission counts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Scavenger Hunt" icon="map">
    **Monthly.** Staff hide clues across the entire world — from the spawn market to the deep wilderness. Follow the trail to claim a coin prize. Hunts vary in difficulty and some clues require solving riddles or completing mini-challenges along the way.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Seasonal & Holiday Events" icon="star">
    **Around major holidays.** DirtShop SMP celebrates Christmas, Halloween, Easter, and other holidays with limited-time events, themed decorations, exclusive cosmetics, and special game modes. Keep an eye on #announcements for exact dates.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Participate

Getting involved in events is straightforward. You don't need to sign up in advance for most events — just show up. Here's how to stay in the loop and get to the action:

- **Discord #events** — all events are announced in the #events channel with full details, start times, and any special rules. React to the announcement to confirm your attendance and get a reminder ping.
- **In-game `/events` command** — type `/events` at any time while in-game to pull up a list of upcoming scheduled events with countdowns.
- **Events arena** — teleport directly to the central events arena by running `/warp events`. Most scheduled events take place here unless otherwise stated in the announcement.

<Tip>
  Assign yourself the **@Events** role in Discord's #roles channel to receive a ping every time a new event is announced or about to begin. It's the easiest way to never miss a Friday PvP Night or a surprise seasonal event.
</Tip>

## Prizes

Winning — or even just participating in — DirtShop SMP events comes with real, tangible rewards:

- **Coins** — the primary prize for most events. Coin amounts scale with the event's difficulty and scope. Scavenger hunt jackpots can be substantial.
- **Exclusive cosmetics** — certain events award cosmetic items like hats, trails, or particle effects that cannot be obtained anywhere else on the server.
- **Temporary rank boosts** — top performers in select events receive a short-term rank upgrade, granting access to higher-tier perks for a limited period.

All prizes are delivered automatically to your in-game account or inventory at the conclusion of the event.

## Suggesting an Event

Have an idea for something the community would love? DirtShop SMP staff actively reads player suggestions. Head to the **#suggestions** channel in Discord and submit your idea with as much detail as possible — the format, how players would participate, and any prize ideas you have in mind. Popular suggestions with strong community support are frequently picked up and added to the events calendar.
