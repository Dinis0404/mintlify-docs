The DirtShop SMP Discord server is the beating heart of the community outside of the game itself. Whether you need help from staff, want to trade items with other players, stay up to date on announcements, or just hang out and chat, Discord is where it all happens. This page walks you through joining, navigating the channels, understanding roles, and using the server's bots.

<Note>
  The Discord rules are separate from — and apply **in addition to** — the in-game server rules. Violating Discord rules can result in punishments that carry over to your in-game account. Make sure you read #rules after joining.
</Note>

## Joining the Discord

Join the DirtShop SMP Discord at **[discord.gg/dirtshop](https://discord.gg/dirtshop)**. Once you're in, you'll land in a read-only welcome area. To unlock all channels and verify your identity as a server player, you need to link your Minecraft account.

**To link your Minecraft account:**
1. Join the Minecraft server and run `/link` in chat. You'll receive a unique verification code.
2. Head to the **#bot-commands** channel in Discord and type `/link <your-minecraft-username>`.
3. The bot will confirm the link and automatically assign you the **Community Member** role, unlocking the full channel list.

## Discord Roles

Roles in the DirtShop SMP Discord serve two purposes: they identify who you are and control what you can see and do. Here are the key roles you'll encounter:

- **Community Member** — the baseline role granted to all verified members. Unlocks access to the majority of channels.
- **VIP / Ranks** — if you hold a purchased or earned rank in-game (e.g., VIP, Elite, Legend), that rank is automatically reflected in Discord once your account is linked. These roles come with colored name tags and access to rank-exclusive Discord channels.
- **@Events** — an opt-in notification role. Assign it to yourself in the #roles channel to receive pings when events are announced or about to start.
- **Staff Roles** — Helper, Moderator, Admin, and Owner roles identify the moderation team. You can ping staff in #help-desk if you need assistance.

## Minecraft Account Linking

Linking your Minecraft account is required to access several channels and is strongly recommended for all players. Beyond unlocking channels, linking your account allows the Discord bot to fetch your in-game data (balance, playtime, rank) and ensures that staff can verify your identity when handling support tickets or ban appeals.

- **In-game command:** `/link` — generates your personal verification code
- **Discord command:** `/link <username>` in #bot-commands — completes the verification
- If your link ever breaks (e.g., after a username change), simply run the process again to re-verify.

## Bot Commands

The DirtShop SMP Discord bot provides a bridge between the game and Discord. You can run the following commands in **#bot-commands** without ever logging into the server:

<Accordion title="Useful Bot Commands">
  | Command | What It Does |
  |---|---|
  | `/balance` | Check your current in-game coin balance |
  | `/playtime` | View your total hours played on the server |
  | `/rank` | See your current in-game rank and its perks |
  | `/link <username>` | Link or re-link your Minecraft account to Discord |
  | `/events` | List upcoming scheduled server events |
  | `/playerinfo <username>` | Look up basic public info on any player |

  All commands are limited to #bot-commands to keep other channels clean. Running bot commands in unrelated channels will result in the message being deleted by the bot.
</Accordion>
