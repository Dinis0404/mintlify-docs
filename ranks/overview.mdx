DirtShop SMP rewards the time and effort you put into the community with a six-tier rank progression system. As you accumulate playtime, pass in-game quizzes, and earn the trust of the community and staff, you'll climb from a fresh-faced **Newcomer** all the way to the coveted **Legend** status — unlocking powerful commands, quality-of-life perks, and cosmetics along the way. Ranks are earned, not bought, which means every badge next to your name represents genuine dedication to the server.

## Rank Progression

The table below shows every rank in order, along with the requirements you need to meet to earn it. All playtime figures refer to active time on the server, tracked automatically.

| Rank | Name | How to Earn |
|------|------|-------------|
| 1 | Newcomer | Automatic on join |
| 2 | Member | 5 hours playtime + rules quiz |
| 3 | Regular | 24 hours playtime |
| 4 | Veteran | 100 hours playtime |
| 5 | Elite | 250 hours playtime + staff review |
| 6 | Legend | 500 hours playtime + community vote |

## Donor Ranks

In addition to the earned rank ladder, DirtShop SMP offers three **Donor Ranks** — **VIP**, **VIP+**, and **MVP** — available for purchase through the store at [store.dirtshop.gg](https://store.dirtshop.gg). Donor ranks exist to help keep the server running and reward supporters with convenience features and cosmetics. They sit alongside your earned rank, not above it.

<Note>
  Donor ranks are strictly cosmetic and convenience-focused. No donor rank grants access to gameplay advantages such as extra damage, additional resources, or protected loot. DirtShop SMP is committed to keeping progression fair for every player, regardless of whether they've spent money on the server.
</Note>
