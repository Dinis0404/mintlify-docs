Every rank you earn on DirtShop SMP comes with a tangible set of perks that make your time on the server more comfortable and expressive. From extra home points and teleport commands in the early ranks to full flight in your land claims and custom join messages at the top, there's always something worth working toward. The table below gives you a complete side-by-side look at what each rank unlocks, so you know exactly what's waiting for you at the next tier.

## Earned Rank Perks

| Perk | Newcomer | Member | Regular | Veteran | Elite | Legend |
|------|:--------:|:------:|:-------:|:-------:|:-----:|:------:|
| Set Homes | 1 | 3 | 5 | 10 | 15 | 20 |
| `/tpa` | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Colored Chat | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| `/fly` (in claims) | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Custom Join Message | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ | ✓ |
| Particle Effects | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ | ✓ |

A few notes on specific perks:

- **Set Homes** — Use `/sethome <name>` to save a location and `/home <name>` to return to it. Your home limit increases with every rank.
- **`/tpa`** — Unlocked at Member, this lets you send a teleport request to any online player with `/tpa <player>`. The receiving player must accept with `/tpaccept`.
- **Colored Chat** — Available from Veteran onward, this lets you use color codes (e.g., `&a`, `&b`) in your chat messages to add a splash of color to your text.
- **`/fly` (in claims)** — Elite and Legend players can fly freely within their own land claims. Flight is automatically disabled when you leave a claim you own.
- **Custom Join Message** — Legends can set a personal message that broadcasts to the server when they log in, configured via `/joinmsg <message>`.
- **Particle Effects** — Legends unlock a selection of ambient particle trails and effects, toggled and customized with `/particles`.

## Donor Rank Perks (VIP / VIP+ / MVP)

Donor ranks supplement your earned rank with convenience features and cosmetics. Each tier builds on the one below it.

<Accordion title="VIP">
  - **`/nick`** — Set a custom display name visible in chat and above your head.
  - **5 extra homes** — Added on top of whatever your earned rank already provides.
  - **Colored chat prefix** — Display a colored `[VIP]` tag before your name in chat.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="VIP+">
  Everything included in **VIP**, plus:
  - **`/fly` everywhere** — Fly in any area of the server, not just inside your own claims.
  - **`/hat`** — Place any block or item on your head as a cosmetic helmet.
  - **`/ptime`** — Set your personal client-side time of day without affecting other players (e.g., `/ptime day` so your screen is always bright).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="MVP">
  Everything included in **VIP+**, plus:
  - **`/god`** — Enables god mode (damage immunity) in PvP-off zones only.
  - **Custom particle trail** — A unique animated particle effect that follows you as you move, customized through `/trail`.
  - **Priority queue** — Skip the regular join queue and connect to a full server faster than non-priority players.
</Accordion>

<Warning>
  `/fly` and `/god` are **automatically disabled** in all designated PvP zones, regardless of your rank or donor status. This applies to every player without exception to ensure PvP remains fair for everyone on the server.
</Warning>

<Note>
  Perks are subject to change as DirtShop SMP updates its plugins and server configuration. When significant changes are made to rank perks, an announcement will be posted in the server Discord. Always check the latest perk list here or in-game with `/rankup info`.
</Note>
