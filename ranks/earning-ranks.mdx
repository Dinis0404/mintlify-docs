Climbing the rank ladder on DirtShop SMP is straightforward — spend time on the server, meet the requirements for your next rank, and claim it. Some ranks are awarded automatically the moment you hit a threshold, while higher ranks involve a short quiz or a staff review to make sure you're a good fit for the added trust they carry. This page walks you through everything you need to know to move from one rank to the next as smoothly as possible.

## Checking Your Progress

Before you can rank up, you need to know where you stand. DirtShop SMP gives you two commands to keep tabs on your progress at any time:

- **`/rankup info`** — Displays your current rank, the name of the next rank, and exactly how much of each requirement you still need to complete (e.g., remaining playtime hours, whether you've passed the quiz).
- **`/playtime`** — Shows your total accumulated playtime on the server in hours and minutes.

Run `/rankup info` regularly as you approach a new rank so you're never left guessing how close you are.

## Automatic vs. Manual Ranks

Not every rank is claimed the same way. Here's how each type works:

**Automatically claimable ranks (Regular, Veteran)** are awarded as soon as you meet the playtime threshold. You still need to run `/rankup` to apply the rank to your account — it won't appear on its own — but no staff involvement is required.

**The Member rank** has one extra step: after reaching 5 hours of playtime, you must pass the rules quiz by running the `/quiz` command in-game. The quiz consists of 10 questions covering DirtShop SMP's core rules. You must answer all 10 correctly to proceed. If you fail, you can retake the quiz after a short cooldown.

**Elite and Legend** require a staff review before they're granted. Staff look at your conduct on the server and in the community as a whole before approving the promotion. These ranks aren't just about hours — they're a recognition of the positive role you play on DirtShop SMP.

## How to Request a Rankup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your requirements">
    Run `/rankup info` to confirm you've met every requirement for your next rank. Make sure both your playtime and any additional requirements (quiz, review) are satisfied before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete all requirements">
    If you still have playtime to accumulate, keep playing! If you need to pass the rules quiz for the Member rank, run `/quiz` and work through all 10 questions. There's no time limit per question, so read carefully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim your rank in-game">
    Once all requirements are met, run `/rankup` in chat. For Newcomer → Member, Regular, and Veteran, this command will immediately apply your new rank and announce it to the server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a Discord ticket for Elite or Legend">
    Elite and Legend ranks require staff approval. Head to the **#rank-requests** channel in the [DirtShop SMP Discord](https://discord.gg/dirtshop) and open a support ticket. Include your in-game username and the rank you're applying for. Staff typically review requests within 24–48 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use `/vote` to vote for DirtShop SMP on server listing sites. Each vote earns you bonus coins and a small XP boost that counts toward your rank progress — it's one of the easiest ways to speed up your journey through the early ranks.
</Tip>

## Donor Ranks

Donor ranks — **VIP**, **VIP+**, and **MVP** — are purchased separately from the earned rank ladder and do not replace it. You can buy a donor rank at [store.dirtshop.gg](https://store.dirtshop.gg) at any time, regardless of your current earned rank.

- Donor ranks are typically applied to your account **within 5 minutes** of a successful purchase.
- If your rank hasn't been applied after 5 minutes, run **`/redeem`** in-game while connected to the server to manually trigger the redemption.
- If `/redeem` doesn't resolve the issue, open a support ticket in Discord with your transaction ID ready.
