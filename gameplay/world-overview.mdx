DirtShop SMP is home to several distinct dimensions and worlds, each serving a different purpose for your survival experience. Whether you're staking out a permanent base, racing through the Nether highway, hunting for End Cities, or stripping a resource world bare for materials, knowing which world to use — and when — is essential to thriving on the server.

## Overworld

The Overworld is the heart of DirtShop SMP and where you'll spend most of your time. This is the permanent world — your base, farms, and builds live here and are protected between server restarts and resets.

- **World Border:** The Overworld extends **10,000 blocks in every direction** from the center (0, 0), giving you a 20,000 × 20,000 block play area.
- **Land Claiming:** Land claiming is active in the Overworld. You must claim your land to protect it from griefing and theft. Unclaimed builds are not protected by the server.
- **Building:** All permanent structures, farms, and player shops should be built in the Overworld.
- **Resource Gathering:** Avoid mining, deforesting, or strip-mining in the Overworld. Use the Resource World instead to keep the landscape clean for everyone.

<Warning>
  Unclaimed land in the Overworld is not protected. Always claim your chunks before logging off, especially after a new build session.
</Warning>

## Nether

The Nether on DirtShop SMP functions like the standard vanilla Nether dimension, with one major community addition: a **Nether highway system built at y=120**.

- **Nether Highway:** Ice roads run along the cardinal directions (North, South, East, West) at y=120, allowing fast boat travel. This is the recommended way to travel long distances.
- **1:8 Scale:** Every block you travel in the Nether equals 8 blocks in the Overworld. Building your Nether portal close to spawn gives you a significant travel advantage.
- **Portal Linking:** To link portals correctly, divide your Overworld coordinates by 8 to find the matching Nether coordinates.
- **Danger:** The Nether is not a protected dimension. PvP rules apply and mobs are active — come prepared.

## The End

The End is accessible to all players and is a great destination for late-game gear and Elytra wings.

- **Access:** Use `/warp end` to reach the End portal at spawn and travel to The End.
- **Main Island:** The central End island contains the Ender Dragon fight. The dragon may already be defeated — check with other players or server staff.
- **Outer Islands:** Travel at least **1,000 blocks** from the main island to reach the outer End islands, where you'll find End Cities, Shulkers, and Elytra in End Ship chests.
- **No claiming:** The End is an open, unclaimable dimension. Any chests or structures you find may already be looted.

## Resource World

The Resource World is a dedicated gathering dimension designed to preserve the beauty of the main Overworld. Use this world for all your mining, quarrying, and bulk resource collection.

- **Access:** Use `/warp resource` to teleport directly to the Resource World.
- **Rules:** Building, land claiming, and permanent structures are **not allowed** in the Resource World.
- **Reset Schedule:** The Resource World is reset on a monthly basis, replenishing all ores, trees, and terrain.
- **What to gather here:** Stone, ores, wood, sand, gravel, clay, and any other raw materials you need in large quantities.

<Note>
  The Resource World is typically reset on the **first Saturday of each month**. Any items or structures left in the Resource World will be permanently deleted on reset. Do not store anything you want to keep in this world — always bring your gathered materials back to the Overworld.
</Note>

## Spawn Area

The Spawn Area is the protected hub of DirtShop SMP, always located at coordinates **0, 0** in the Overworld. You can return here at any time by typing `/spawn`.

- **Protection:** The Spawn Area is fully protected — no building, breaking, or PvP is allowed in this zone.
- **Server Shop:** Browse and sell items at the server shop located within spawn.
- **Warp Board:** Find a community warp board listing all public warps and points of interest on the server.
- **Info Signs:** New to the server? Read the info signs around spawn for rules, tips, and server announcements.
- **Quick Access Commands:**

| Command | Description |
|---|---|
| `/spawn` | Teleport to the Spawn Area |
| `/warp shop` | Open the server shop |
| `/warp resource` | Travel to the Resource World |
| `/warp end` | Travel to The End portal |
| `/warp market` | Visit the player market area |
