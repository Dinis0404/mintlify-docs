Whether you just joined for the first time or you've been playing for months, these general commands are the backbone of your daily experience on DirtShop SMP. From navigating the world and checking your playtime to managing your chat and staying up to date with the rules, every command listed here is available to all players the moment they log in.

## Command Reference

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/spawn` | Teleport to the server spawn |
| `/help` | Open the in-game help menu |
| `/rules` | View the server rules |
| `/playtime` | Check your total playtime |
| `/afk` | Mark yourself as AFK |
| `/msg <player> <message>` | Send a private message |
| `/r <message>` | Reply to last private message |
| `/ignore <player>` | Block messages from a player |
| `/list` | View all online players |
| `/seen <player>` | Check when a player was last online |
| `/rtp` | Random teleport to a safe location |
| `/vote` | Open the voting links for rewards |
| `/ranks` | View available ranks |
| `/quiz` | Take the rules quiz (required for Member rank) |
| `/report <player>` | Report a rule-breaking player to staff |
| `/helpop <message>` | Send an urgent message directly to all online staff |

<Warning>
  Use `/report <player>` to flag a player who is breaking the rules. Staff review all reports, even if no one is online to respond immediately. For urgent situations where you need a live response, use `/helpop <message>` to broadcast your message to every staff member currently on the server.
</Warning>

<Info>
  New to DirtShop SMP? Run `/rules` and then `/quiz` to pass the rules quiz and unlock the **Member** rank, which gives you access to additional commands and features across the server.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How do I get OP?">
  DirtShop SMP does not use OP for staff or players. All permissions are role-based — staff members have elevated permissions tied to their rank, not operator status. If you need assistance, use `/msg` to reach an online staff member or open a ticket in our Discord.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change my username?">
  Username changes are tied directly to your Minecraft account. If you change your username through Mojang, your new name will be recognised on the server automatically on your next login. Your stats, homes, and balance are all preserved.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why am I in spectator mode?">
  Finding yourself in spectator mode is almost always a bug. You shouldn't be able to enter spectator mode as a regular player. Contact a staff member using `/msg <staffname> <message>` or reach out in the Discord support channel so they can restore you to survival mode as quickly as possible.
</Accordion>
