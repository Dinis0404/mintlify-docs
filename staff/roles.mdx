The DirtShop SMP staff team keeps the server safe, fair, and enjoyable for everyone. Each role in the hierarchy carries distinct responsibilities, and knowing who does what helps you get the right help faster. Whether you need to report a griefer, appeal a ban, or ask a basic question, there's always someone on the team suited to assist you.

## Staff Hierarchy

| Role | Badge | Responsibilities |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Helper | `[Helper]` | First point of contact; answers player questions; monitors chat |
| Moderator | `[Mod]` | Issues warnings, mutes, kicks, and temp bans; investigates reports |
| Senior Moderator | `[Sr. Mod]` | Reviews ban appeals; mentors Helpers; handles escalated issues |
| Admin | `[Admin]` | Manages plugins, permissions, and world settings; handles permanent bans |
| Owner | `[Owner]` | Final authority on all decisions; server infrastructure and payments |

## How to Identify Staff

Staff members are easy to spot both in-game and in Discord. In chat, every staff member displays a colored name tag along with their badge prefix — for example, `[Mod] PlayerName` — so you always know who you're speaking with at a glance.

To see a list of all staff members currently online, run `/staff` from anywhere in-game. The command will display each online staff member alongside their role, making it simple to find the right person for your situation.

<Tip>
  Use `/helpop <message>` to send a private message to all online staff at once. This is the fastest way to flag an urgent issue — every staff member currently logged in will see your message immediately.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Staff members will **never** ask for your password, account credentials, or any personal account details. If anyone claiming to be staff requests this information, report them to an Admin or Owner immediately via a Discord ticket.
</Note>
